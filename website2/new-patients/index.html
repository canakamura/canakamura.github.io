Looking for a Kids Dentist?

If you’re searching for a kids dentist in or around Mililani, we’d love for you to consider our local pediatric dental office. Unlike other family dental offices that happen to treat children in addition to adults, Honu Smiles Pediatric Dentistry is completely devoted to kids and their needs.

For starters, our doctor, Dr. JW Chang, is a pediatric dentist. That means that after finishing dental school, he completed an additional 2-year residency program in pediatric dentistry. Instead of just treating his patients like little adults, he is sensitive to the unique dental issues that kids face and how best to work with them so they feel calm, safe, and comfortable. Our office also has been specifically designed to be a fun, welcoming place for children with our prize toy machine, video games, cavity-free club program, and more.

When it comes to your child’s health and their smile, trust an expert. We hope to see you and your family soon at Honu Smiles Pediatric Dentistry!

New Patient Forms

We kindly require that our new patient forms be filled out for all first-time visitors. This information helps us adjust our care to your child’s needs and lets us know of any other conditions that might affect their dental health.

You are more than welcome to fill out these forms ahead of time by clicking the button below. Just don’t forget to bring out the completed forms to your child’s first appointment! If you’d rather complete them in our office, we ask that you please arrive 10 to 15 minutes early so we can make sure to keep your scheduled appointment time.
