    New Patients

    Welcome to Nakamura Children's Dentistry

    Your child’s first visit to our practice is a very important milestone. Our staff’s goal is to “Build Healthy Smiles” and establish a lasting relationship with you and your child. Since each child is unique, we will always create a personalized treatment for each patient. We hope that your child’s visits are fun, educational and provide a strong foundation for a lifetime of positive dental experiences.


    At the first visit, we will:

    ♥ Give you and your child an opportunity to meet Dr. Chris and answer any questions you may have

    ♥ Review your child’s overall health and oral health including their dental growth and development, cavity risk factors and trauma/accident prevention

    ♥ Exam/clean your child’s teeth and apply fluoride. If your child is very young, your child will be on your lap in a “knee to knee position” so that your child can be close to you while we examine and provide treatment

    ♥ Demonstrate to both parents and child how to properly brush and floss your child’s teeth

    ♥ Take x-rays, if necessary and age appropriate

    ♥ Review previous home care habits and discuss brushing and flossing, diet, and safe fluoride use

    ♥ Discuss potential dental treatment needs or referrals for your child

    ♥ Positively reinforce cooperative behavior throughout the appointment
