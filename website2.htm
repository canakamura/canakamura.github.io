Welcome to Honu Smiles Pediatric Dentistry in Mililani, HI!

At Honu Smiles, we are all about creating a fun, friendly environment where children of all ages — from infants to teens — can get the dental care they need for a lifetime of smiles. We are conveniently located at Mililani Mauka Gateway, and we’d love to have you and your family stop by sometime soon!

About Dr. JW Chang

Dr. JW Chang is a Hawaii native, having been born and raised in Honolulu. He earned his Doctorate of Dental Surgery from the University of Washington School of Dentistry and then went on to complete a two-year residency in Pediatric Dentistry at Children’s Mercy Hospital – University of Missouri Kansas City. Dr. JW is a board certified Diplomate of the American Board of Pediatric Dentistry.
